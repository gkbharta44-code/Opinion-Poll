Overview & Mission

What Delper Ecom is and why it exists

Delper Ecom Pvt Ltd (India) and its global vehicle CryptoCard Global are the companies behind the DPR (Delper) blockchain-native loyalty and settlement protocol. The platform transforms every point-of-sale (POS) transaction and supply chain payment into a self-compounding reward ecosystem backed by Ethereum (ETH). Every time a business sells something, every tier of the supply chain automatically earns DPR tokens — without any extra work or cost.
Three core problems:
  • Fragmented Loyalty: Global loyalty programmes lose $500B+ in unredeemed value annually with no interoperability between merchants.
  • Supply Chain Opacity: $120 trillion in annual supply chain transactions flow through slow, costly intermediaries with no shared tokenised settlement layer.
  • No Shared Upside: Merchants, distributors, and manufacturers all transact but none benefit from network growth — value only flows to payment rails.
Delper solves all three by making every transaction reward all participants simultaneously.

DPR Token Economics

Token price, supply, rewards, and how it all works

DPR is a utility token with two primary functions:
  • Loyalty Reward: Earned by consumers and supply chain participants on every qualifying transaction. Redeemable at any POS terminal for goods and services.
  • Supply Chain Settlement: Used by supply chain participants across all 6 tiers to settle procurement obligations using DPR instead of traditional currency.
Supporting 38 fiat currencies and 31 cryptocurrencies, DPR is designed to work globally across any merchant or supplier.
The total supply is 1.5 trillion (1.5T) DPR tokens.
  • Seed price: $0.21 per token
  • Y0 (launch) price: $244.84 per token
  • Y5 (5-year) projected price: $327.40 per token
Each standard POS transaction of $247 generates 2.94 DPR tokens as a loyalty reward (75% of the transaction value ÷ token price).
Every $247 standard transaction is automatically split by a smart contract:
  • 25% = $61.75 Ad Discount — immediate savings for the consumer.
  • 75% = $185.25 Loyalty Reward → converted to 2.94 DPR tokens, credited instantly to the buyer's Hyperledger wallet.
This split is enforced by an immutable smart contract and requires no manual action.
  • Rewards (BNB/passive income): Can be withdrawn from Month 10 onwards.
  • DPR token sale: Eligible holders can sell DPR from the 60th month (5 years) from their date of purchase.
  • POS Redemption: Tokens earned as loyalty rewards can be redeemed at any partnered POS terminal at any time for goods and services.
These lock-up terms are enforced via smart contract.
The DPR token is backed by an ETH Stability Pool:
  • 80% of all platform charges are directed into an ETH treasury every month.
  • If DPR price drops, the smart contract automatically triggers ETH buybacks, creating a price floor.
  • By Year 5, the pool holds $17.51 trillion in ETH, backing each of the 1.5T tokens with $11.67 in hard ETH collateral.
  • After risk mitigation, the reserve is diversified: 40% USDC/USDT, 25% US T-Bills, 15% ETH, 10% BTC, 10% Gold-backed tokens.

Business Model

How Delper Ecom generates revenue

The platform operates four charge streams, each at 0.5%:
  • Reward Earned Charge: 0.5% on the total loyalty pool when tokens are credited ($7.7B/month).
  • Reward Redeemed Charge: 0.5% on redemption value when tokens are spent at POS ($7.7B/month).
  • SC Buy Charge: 0.5% when supply chain participants buy DPR to settle procurement ($145.3B/month).
  • SC Sell Charge: 0.5% when supply chain participants liquidate DPR received for goods/services ($145.3B/month).
Total: $306B/month at launch. 80% ($244.8B) feeds the ETH pool; 20% ($61.2B) is retained as operating revenue.
When a POS transaction occurs, smart contracts automatically propagate rewards upstream through all 6 supply chain layers at a recursive 0.85× rate per layer: L1 Consumer 1.000× · L2 Distributor 0.850× · L3 Manufacturer 0.723× · L4 Processor 0.614× · L5 Wholesaler 0.522× · L6 Raw Materials 0.444×. The sum = 4.15×, meaning $1 of reward at the POS generates $4.15 worth of total ecosystem reward — at zero extra acquisition cost.
For everyday consumers:
  • Users watch ads tailored to their interests and earn tokens at ₹12/minute × 180 minutes/day.
  • Tokens are redeemable for groceries, household essentials, and more through the Delper App.
  • Vendors pay an 18–25% commission per order fulfilled through the platform.
  • A Token-Linked Card (Visa/Mastercard) enables real-time INR token conversion at any merchant globally.
The model targets $247/month in purchasing power per family.

Technology & Architecture

The blockchain and infrastructure stack powering DPR

DPR is built on IBM Hyperledger Fabric for enterprise transactions, with Ethereum mainnet as the settlement and treasury layer. Chainlink Oracles provide real-time price feeds. The smart contract layer has been audited by Trail of Bits and ConsenSys Diligence. KYC is powered by Jumio, covering 200 countries and 5,000+ document types, FATF Travel Rule compliant.
Supply chain settlement targets 90 seconds — compared to the 30–120 day industry standard for cross-border trade finance. Consumer POS rewards are credited instantly at point of purchase via the Hyperledger smart contract layer.
Each verified user receives a Hyperledger wallet supporting 38 fiat currencies and 31 cryptocurrencies, protected by ISO 27001-certified multi-layer encryption with private keys stored in a hardware security module (HSM). A Token-Linked Card (Visa/Mastercard integrated) enables real-time token conversion at any merchant worldwide. Mobile biometric unlock and 2FA are standard.

Supply Chain Protocol

How DPR integrates with global procurement and logistics

Supply chain participants use DPR to buy and sell goods and services using the token as the settlement currency instead of traditional fiat — eliminating the need for letters of credit, trade finance intermediaries, and slow bank-to-bank transfers. Every procurement transaction triggers automatic DPR rewards to all 6 upstream tiers.
Integration happens through existing procurement platforms:
  • If your company uses SAP Ariba or Coupa, the DPR settlement layer integrates directly into your existing workflow — no new software required.
  • For logistics and trade finance, integration is available via Contour, Marco Polo, project44, or Flexport.
  • Direct API integration via the Hyperledger smart contract layer is available for enterprise-grade custom deployments.
